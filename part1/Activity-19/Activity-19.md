# Activity A19: Join a CS/DS/cybersecurity club

## Objective
To engage with a cybersecurity-related community and explore collaborative learning opportunities.

## Methodology
I joined the University Computer Club (UCC) at the beginning of the semester. I accessed the club through its online platform (Discord) and observed discussions among members.

## Findings

### 1. Membership
I successfully joined UCC, which is a student community focused on computing and cybersecurity topics.

## Analysis
Joining a cybersecurity-related club provides access to a collaborative learning environment. Even minimal participation allows exposure to real-world issues, such as troubleshooting SSL problems.

This highlights the importance of community engagement in developing cybersecurity knowledge.
